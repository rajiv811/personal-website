About

A film enthusiast at heart.

Hi! I'm Rajiv and I am a film enthusiast and freelance photographer / videographer. The first camera I ever owned was a Canon AE-1 that was passed down to me from my brother, a relic of time that survived half a century. Little did I know that this bulky, beat up camera would become the genesis of a never-ending love of film.

I have since shot film for friends and family and recently had the honor of photographing a wedding proposal. If you want to collaborate on a project, please don't hesitate to reach out.
