About

A film enthusiast at heart.

I'm Rajiv, a photographer and filmmaker who fell for film long before I understood why. As a young boy I was handed down an old film camera, and something about it stuck. The weight of it, the deliberate pace, the quiet anticipation between pressing the shutter and finally seeing what you'd captured. I fell in love with the natural grain of film, the texture, the softness, the imperfections that make a frame feel alive rather than manufactured.

That first camera turned into a lifelong obsession with analog formats. I shoot 35mm and medium format for stills, and I chase the same feeling in motion through 8mm and 16mm film. Each format has its own character: the intimacy of 35mm, the depth and detail of medium format, the nostalgic flicker of small-gauge motion film.
